Tub Surrounds

From precision tile work to custom glass door installation—we provide a complete, turnkey transformation for your bathroom.

Modernizing the Classic Tub Surround

A tub surround is often the centerpiece of a guest or family bathroom. In many Minnesota homes, these areas are prone to moisture issues due to failing caulk or porous grout. At True Craft Tile & Stone, we specialize in high-performance tub surrounds that combine aesthetic beauty with industrial-grade waterproofing.

We handle every phase of the project. Whether you are keeping your existing tub or upgrading to a new cast iron or acrylic model, we ensure the interface between the tub and the wall is perfectly sealed and structurally sound.

Complete Glass & Hardware Solutions

Why deal with multiple contractors? Unlike many tile installers who walk away after the grout is dry, we offer **full-service installation**, including your glass enclosure. We install everything from classic sliding bypass doors to high-end frameless pivot glass.

Waterproofing That Lasts

Even though a tub surround doesn't face the same constant water pressure as a shower floor, it still requires expert protection. We utilize high-quality backer systems and waterproofing membranes to prevent moisture from reaching your studs. This ensures that your new tile work remains mold-free and beautiful for decades.

Investment: A Worthy Upgrade

A professional tub surround renovation in the Twin Cities typically starts at **$4,500**, depending on the size and complexity. Adding features like custom niches, intricate mosaic accents, or premium glass doors will adjust the final quote. We provide transparent, itemized estimates so you know exactly where your investment is going.
