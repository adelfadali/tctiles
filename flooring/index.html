Our Flooring Specialties

01 / Porcelain & Ceramic Tile

Ceramic Tile: Crafted from natural clay and fired at high temperatures. It is the ideal choice for decorative bathroom floors and kitchen backsplashes. It offers a softer feel underfoot than porcelain but remains highly durable for residential use.

Porcelain Tile: The "heavyweight" champion. Fired at much higher temperatures, it is denser and frost-proof. We specialize in Large Format Porcelain (LFP), which reduces grout lines and creates a high-end, seamless look.

  • • Zero-Lippage mechanical leveling used on all installs.
  • • High-performance modified thinsets for a lifetime bond.
  • • Ideal for radiant heated floor systems.

Technical Fact

Porcelain has a water absorption rate of < 0.5%, making it effectively waterproof and stain-proof for life.

Subfloor Secret

We use self-leveling underlayment for every LVP install. Without a perfectly flat floor, "click-lock" joints can snap over time.

02 / Vinyl Planks & Laminate

Luxury Vinyl Planks (LVP): 100% waterproof. Modern LVP uses a rigid SPC core that doesn't expand like wood. It is the perfect solution for families with pets and active households.

Laminated Flooring: Known for its incredible scratch resistance and authentic wood texture. Modern laminate is highly water-resistant and offers a "depth" of color that mimics real hardwood perfectly.

  • • 1/4" perimeter expansion gaps for MN humidity shifts.
  • • Premium underlayment for sound dampening and comfort.
  • • Precision transitions at every doorway and staircase.
