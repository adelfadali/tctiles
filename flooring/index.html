Engineering a Floor for a Lifetime

In the Twin Cities, a tile floor is subject to massive thermal stress. Many homeowners see grout cracking within 2 years of a remodel. This is almost always due to improper **subfloor preparation**. As a specialist **tile installer in Shakopee**, we don't just lay tile on wood. We build a multi-layered system designed to decouple the tile from the home's movement.

The Subfloor Rule

We utilize Schluter-DITRA uncoupling membranes. This layer allows the subfloor to expand and contract while your tile stays 100% bonded and crack-free.

Lippage Control

Using Italian-made mechanical leveling systems, we ensure every tile is perfectly flush with the next. No "tripping hazards," just a smooth glass-like finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a new tile floor installation take?

Typically, a standard 200 sq. ft. room takes 4 to 6 days. This includes demolition, subfloor leveling, uncoupling membrane installation, tile setting, and 24 hours for grout curing.

Can you install tile over my existing flooring?

As a professional **flooring contractor**, we strongly advise against it. To guarantee our lifetime warranty, we must inspect the subfloor and bond directly to the structural base.
