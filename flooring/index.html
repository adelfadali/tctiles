Flooring Material Encyclopedia

Ceramic Tile

The classic choice for bathroom walls and decorative floors. Ceramic is highly customizable and cost-effective, offering a beautiful glazed finish for residential style.

Porcelain Tile

The "Lifetime" material. Denser than ceramic and fired at 2,200°F. It is nearly 100% waterproof and scratch-proof—the gold standard for high-traffic Minneapolis homes.

Vinyl Planks (LVP)

100% Waterproof Rigid Core flooring. Ideal for basements and mudrooms. We install LVP with premium subfloor leveling to ensure zero "bouncing" or clicking joints.

Laminated Wood

The most realistic wood-look with extreme scratch resistance (AC-Ratings). Perfect for households with pets that want the warmth of wood with modern durability.
