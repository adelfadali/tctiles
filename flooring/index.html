Our Flooring Specialties

Expertise in Every Material

01. Ceramic Tile

Ceramic tile is the time-tested favorite for residential aesthetics. It is crafted from natural clays and glazed for a moisture-resistant finish. While slightly softer than porcelain, its versatility allows for incredible design flexibility in pattern-heavy rooms.

  • • Best for Bathroom Walls & Floors
  • • Cost-Effective Luxury Aesthetic
  • • Superior for Decorative Mosaics
  • • High Coefficient of Friction (Slip-Resistance)

02. Porcelain Tile

Porcelain is the ultimate investment for durability. Fired at higher temperatures than ceramic, it is nearly impervious to water (absorption < 0.5%). We specialize in **Large Format Porcelain (LFP)**, requiring Italian mechanical leveling systems for a perfectly flush finish.

  • • Engineered for High-Traffic Areas
  • • 100% Frost & Water Resistant
  • • Scratch-Proof & Stain-Proof Surfaces
  • • Ideal for DITRA-HEAT Systems

03. Vinyl Planks (LVP)

Luxury Vinyl Plank is the modern solution for busy Minnesota households. Using a Rigid SPC Core, LVP is **100% waterproof** and pet-friendly. We ensure a premium install by focusing on the subfloor flatness—eliminating the "hollow" sound common with cheap installs.

  • • 100% Waterproof - Great for Mudrooms
  • • Soft & Warm Underfoot
  • • Incredible Sound Dampening
  • • Impact Resistant SPC Core

04. Laminated Flooring

Modern Laminate offers the most authentic wood texture and depth of color. With high AC-Ratings (Abrasion Class), laminate is built to withstand heavy foot traffic and furniture movement without surface wear.

  • • Superior Scratch Resistance
  • • Deep Embossed Wood Textures
  • • High-Density Fiber Core
  • • Expansion-Grade Perimeter Layout
