Our Flooring Specialties

01 / Ceramic Tile

A timeless choice for light-to-medium traffic. Ceramic is softer and ideal for intricate decorative patterns in bathrooms and mudrooms.

  • #Versatile
  • #Decorative
  • #BathroomReady

02 / Porcelain Tile

The gold standard for durability. Dense, frost-proof, and nearly impervious to water. We specialize in **Large Format Porcelain (LFP)** for a seamless stone look.

  • #IndustrialGrade
  • #ScratchProof
  • #LargeFormat

03 / Luxury Vinyl Planks (LVP)

100% waterproof "Rigid Core" flooring. We ensure a superior finish by applying self-leveling compounds to eliminate "clicking" or "bouncing" underfoot.

  • #Waterproof
  • #PetFriendly
  • #LevelSubfloor

04 / Laminated Flooring

High-performance aesthetics with the authentic texture of natural wood. We focus on critical expansion gaps to prevent buckling during humid summer months.

  • #RealisticTexture
  • #HighACRating
  • #LivingSpace
