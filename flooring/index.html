Expert installation of Ceramic, Porcelain, Laminated, and Vinyl Planks. We don't just lay floors; we engineer them for a lifetime.

01 / Residential Classic

Ceramic Tile

Ceramic tile is a versatile choice for homeowners seeking style and affordability. Created from natural clay, it is ideal for vertical surfaces and medium-traffic areas.

Technical Detail: We focus on the **Coefficient of Friction** to ensure slip-resistance in bathrooms and kitchens.
02 / High-Performance

Porcelain Tile

Fired at extreme temperatures, porcelain is denser and harder than ceramic. It is nearly 100% waterproof and scratch-proof, making it the gold standard for Minnesota homes.

True Craft Specialization: Large Format Porcelain (LFP) with zero-lippage mechanical leveling.
03 / 100% Waterproof

Vinyl Planks (LVP)

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) offers the look of hardwood with total moisture protection. Our LVP features a **Rigid SPC Core** to prevent buckling and denting.

The Standard: We use self-leveling compounds to eliminate "clicking" sounds and ensure a perfectly flat installation.
04 / High-Durability

Laminated Wood

Modern laminate provides an authentic wood texture and extreme scratch resistance. Perfect for high-traffic living areas with pets and children.

Durability: We utilize AC4 and AC5 rated laminates to ensure your floor withstands years of heavy use.
