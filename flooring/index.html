Material Guide 01

Ceramic Tile Installation

Ceramic tile remains the quintessential choice for residential style. Created from natural red or white clay and finished with a durable glaze, it offers an infinite variety of colors and textures. At True Craft, we focus on the **COF (Coefficient of Friction)** to ensure your bathroom or kitchen floor is beautiful yet safe.

Best Use Case:

Perfect for bathroom floors, decorative entryways, and kitchen backsplashes where intricate design and budget-efficiency meet.

Material Guide 02

Porcelain: The Lifetime Floor

Porcelain is the highest evolution of tile. Fired at extreme temperatures (2,200°F+), it becomes a dense, frost-proof slab. We specialize in **Large Format Porcelain (LFP)**, requiring specialized suction-handling equipment and vibration-setting to ensure 100% mortar coverage.

  • Impervious to water (<0.5% absorption)
  • Rated for extreme residential and commercial foot traffic
  • The best conductor for Radiant Heating systems

Luxury Vinyl Planks (LVP)

LVP has become the standard for Minnesota mudrooms and basements. It offers a 100% waterproof "Rigid Core" (SPC) that resists snow, slush, and pet accidents. We provide a **Master-Level Click Install**, ensuring all expansion joints are perfectly hidden under baseboards.

Laminated Wood Flooring

Modern high-definition laminate offers a scratch resistance (AC-Rating) that even natural wood cannot match. It is the perfect choice for living areas where the warm look of hardwood is desired but the durability of a composite is required. We utilize premium underlayments to provide a solid, non-hollow sound underfoot.

Flooring FAQ

How do you prepare the subfloor?

We evaluate the deflection (flex) of the joists. For tile, we ensure it meets L/360 standards and use self-leveling compounds to ensure a variance of no more than 1/8" over 10 feet.

Do you provide removal of old flooring?

Yes. We provide full-service demolition including dust-controlled removal of old tile, carpet, and hardwood, followed by proper disposal and site prep.
