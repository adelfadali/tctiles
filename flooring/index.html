Ceramic Tile Specialties

Ceramic tile is the most popular choice for residential homeowners seeking a balance of beauty and value. It is made from natural clays and minerals, fired in a kiln, and glazed for protection.

Ideal Locations:

Bathrooms, backsplashes, and decorative accent floors. It is lightweight and perfect for vertical and horizontal transitions.

Technical Info:

Ceramic is slightly softer than porcelain, making it easier to cut for intricate patterns like Herringbone or Mosaic layouts.

Porcelain Tile: High-Performance

Porcelain is a denser, tougher version of ceramic. It is fired at much higher temperatures, making it nearly waterproof and scratch-proof. This is the "lifetime" flooring option.

  • ✔ Large Format Porcelain (LFP) Specialist
  • ✔ Impervious to moisture (Great for MN Snow)
  • ✔ Compatible with Heated Flooring Systems

Luxury Vinyl Planks (LVP)

100% Waterproof and incredibly resilient. LVP is the king of modern home renovations in Minnesota. It mimics the look of real wood without the risk of water damage.

The True Craft Standard:

Most contractors skip subfloor prep for LVP. We use self-leveling compounds on every job to ensure your floor is perfectly flat—no "bouncing" or "clicking" joints.

Laminated Wood Flooring

Laminate offers the most realistic wood texture and depth. Modern laminates are highly water-resistant and feature advanced AC-Ratings (Abrasion Class), making them nearly impossible to scratch with pets or furniture.

Note: We utilize high-quality sound-dampening underlayments to ensure your laminate floor feels solid and quiet, never hollow.
