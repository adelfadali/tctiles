Beyond the Surface:
Structural Integrity.

Most flooring failures occur because the installer focused on the tile rather than the subfloor. In Minnesota, wood-framed homes move constantly due to humidity shifts. Without addressing **Deflection (L/360)**, your tile will eventually crack.

Subfloor Preparation

We evaluate the joist span and subfloor thickness to ensure it meets TCNA (Tile Council of North America) standards before a single tile is set.

Self-Leveling Systems

We specialize in high-flow underlayments to ensure a perfectly flat floor (FF-Factor) essential for modern large-format tiles.

Large Format Porcelain Specialists

Modern design trends favor massive tiles (24"x48", 48"x48", and even gauged porcelain panels). These require specialized tools—suction cup rails, vibration machines, and high-performance mortars with high "Transfer" rates. As a leading **tile installer in Shakopee**, we have invested in the Italian technology necessary to handle these materials without breakage or lippage.

The Material Guide

01 / Porcelain & Ceramic

The standard for durability. From wood-look planks to marble-look slabs, porcelain offers the highest resistance to scratches and moisture. Ideal for high-traffic entryways and mudrooms.

02 / Natural Stone

Nothing matches the authenticity of Marble, Slate, or Travertine. We provide expert "Back-Buttering" and high-grade impregnating sealers to protect your stone's natural variation from the elements.

03 / Luxury Mosaics

Detailed patterns and glass tile work for powder rooms or feature floor areas. We use epoxy grouts (Laticrete SpectraLock) to ensure grout lines never stain or discolor.
