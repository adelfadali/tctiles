Advanced Flooring Engineering

As a premier **flooring contractor**, we know that a tile floor is only as good as the subfloor beneath it. In Minnesota, the extreme temperature shifts can cause standard tile installations to crack. That is why **True Craft Tile & Stone** utilizes uncoupling membranes like **Schluter-DITRA** on every project. This ensures your floor can handle the natural movement of your home without ever cracking a tile.

Our Flooring Specialties

Large Format Porcelain (LFP)

Specializing in tiles 24x48 and larger. We use mechanical leveling systems to guarantee a **lippage-free** (completely flat) surface across the entire room.

Heated Flooring Systems

Certified installers of the **DITRA-HEAT** system. We provide luxury warmth combined with structural uncoupling for the ultimate bathroom or kitchen floor.

Natural Stone & Marble

Expert handling of Travertine, Slate, and Marble. We manage the specific mortar and sealing needs required for natural stone to prevent staining and wear.

The Precision Installation Process

We follow a strict 4-step protocol for every **tile installer in Shakopee** project we handle:

  • Subfloor Prep: We grind high concrete spots and use self-leveling compounds to ensure a perfect base.
  • Layout Engineering: We calculate grout lines to ensure perfect symmetry and avoid "sliver cuts" at the edges.
  • Bond Strength: We use high-performance modified thinsets from Laticrete and Mapei for a lifetime bond.
  • Final Seal: We offer professional-grade sealing and epoxy grout options for stain-proof durability.
