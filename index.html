The True Craft Standard

For over 35 years, True Craft Tile and Stone has been the definitive choice for homeowners and businesses in Shakopee, Minneapolis, and across the Twin Cities metro area. Our philosophy is simple: tile is not just a surface; it is a permanent architectural investment that defines the character of your home. We approach every kitchen backsplash, custom shower, and tub surround with the precision of master craftsmen, ensuring that every line is perfectly leveled and every joint is flawlessly grouted.

What sets True Craft Tile and Stone apart is our commitment to modern technical excellence. We are Schluter-Kerdi Authorized installers, specializing in system-based waterproofing. While many contractors still use outdated methods that lead to mold growth and leaking behind walls, we utilize the Kerdi-Board system to create a 100% vapor-tight environment. This means your spa-like custom shower is not just beautiful on the day of installation—it is built to remain mold-free and structurally sound for a lifetime.

Our experience extends beyond residential interiors. We have successfully completed numerous commercial projects, providing high-durability tile solutions for businesses that demand both aesthetic appeal and long-term performance. Whether it is a high-traffic restaurant kitchen or a luxury building lobby, our team manages the complexities of large-scale layouts with the same attention to detail we bring to a private master bath.

In addition to new installations, True Craft Tile and Stone provides expert maintenance and restoration services. Grout is the most vulnerable part of any tile installation; over time, it can crack, stain, or lose its seal. We offer deep-cleaning and regrouting services that can make a ten-year-old bathroom look brand new. For natural stone surfaces like marble, granite, and travertine, our professional sealing techniques protect the porous stone from staining and etching, preserving its natural elegance for years to come.

Furthermore, our flooring expertise covers a vast array of materials and patterns. From high-end porcelain planks that mimic hardwood to classic natural stone tiles, we ensure your flooring is installed with a zero-lippage guarantee. This precision is vital not only for the look of your home but also for the safety and longevity of the material. A perfectly flat floor prevents tiles from cracking under pressure and ensures that your furniture sits perfectly level.

When you choose True Craft Tile and Stone, you aren't just hiring a laborer; you are partnering with a master contractor who understands the chemistry of thin-sets, the physics of substrate preparation, and the artistry of layout design. We provide a 2-year workmanship warranty on all projects, giving you total peace of mind that your home is in the hands of the best in the business.

Schluter waterproof systems, exclusively installed by True Craft Tile & Stone, represent a revolutionary improvement in tile installation technology. Traditional tile methods often rely on cement boards and plastic liners that provide limited waterproofing and are susceptible to leaks and mold growth. Schluter’s systems integrate waterproof membranes, drains, and substrate boards specifically engineered to create a continuous waterproof barrier beneath tile settings. This comprehensive system effectively eliminates water intrusion behind walls and floors, thereby preventing structural damage and health hazards related to mold. For homeowners, this means that their newly installed tile showers, floors, or tub surrounds remain durable and hygienic for decades without costly repairs. Additionally, Schluter systems simplify the installation process of complex wet areas, ensuring all transitions and corners are sealed correctly, and mitigating installation errors common in traditional methods. True Craft’s mastery and authorization with Schluter products guarantee professional application of this premium technology, protecting your home’s investment and ending worries about hidden water damage once and for all.

Tile repair and maintenance are critical services offered by True Craft Tile & Stone to extend the life and beauty of your tile installations. We address common issues such as cracked tiles, loose grout, discoloration, and water damage by thoroughly assessing the problem and applying industry-tested restoration techniques. Our team carefully removes damaged sections while preserving surrounding areas, replaces tiles matching color and texture precisely, and refreshes grout lines for seamless aesthetic restoration. We also provide grout sealing services to prevent future staining and moisture infiltration, helping maintain a hygienic and vibrant surface. Cleanup protocols are strictly followed ensuring no residue or dust is left behind. True Craft’s commitment to quality in repair and maintenance frees homeowners and businesses from concerns over tile deterioration, making your investment in your interior or exterior surfaces last for years to come with professional care and support.

Grout sealing plays an essential role in protecting your tile surfaces by creating a barrier that repels water, dirt, and stains. True Craft Tile & Stone emphasizes professional grout sealing as a maintenance standard to preserve the appearance and durability of your tiled areas. Sealers prevent mold and mildew growth within porous grout lines, reduce discoloration, and make routine cleaning easier. Applying a high-quality, penetrating sealer correctly requires expert surface preparation and understanding of grout types. True Craft uses premium sealants designed for the specific grout installed, ensuring optimal protection without altering tile aesthetics. We recommend periodic sealing depending on usage and environment, often every 1-3 years, to maintain ongoing defense against moisture and wear. Our sealing service extends the lifespan of your tile investment, improves hygiene, and keeps your tile installations vibrant and beautiful for years.
