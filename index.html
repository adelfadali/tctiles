The True Craft Standard of Excellence

We understand that tile isn't just a surface—it's a permanent architectural feature of your home. Unlike generic contractors, we approach every project with the precision of an artisan and the technical knowledge of a master installer.

Why Precision Matters in Tile Installation

Tile installation is a science as much as it is an art. A poorly installed shower can lead to catastrophic water damage behind your walls, often not visible until it’s too late. That is why we specialize in the Schluter-Kerdi Waterproofing System. By using industry-leading vapor management and waterproofing technology, we ensure your bathroom retreat remains beautiful and mold-free for decades.

Our expertise extends beyond the bathroom. From custom kitchen backsplashes that serve as the focal point of your home to large-format porcelain flooring that requires a perfectly level subfloor, we handle the technical challenges that others shy away from. We evaluate every substrate for structural integrity and use premium mortars and grouts tailored to your specific tile material—whether it’s natural marble, glass mosaic, or handmade ceramic.
