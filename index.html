Kitchen Mastery

The kitchen is the heart of your home, and the backsplash is its focal point. Whether you are installing natural stone, glass, or ceramic, we ensure every cut is precise around outlets and cabinetry. From Shakopee to Minneapolis, our kitchen transformations are known for their flawless finish and lasting value.

Bathroom Retreats

A master bathroom should feel like a luxury spa. We specialize in custom shower pans, curbless entries, and full-room waterproofing using advanced Schluter systems. In the Minneapolis climate, moisture control is critical; we ensure your bathroom is built to withstand humidity without ever risking mold or structural decay. We install large-format porcelain, marble slabs, and custom niche shelving that looks built-in, not added on. Our expertise includes heated floor systems (Ditra-Heat) that provide incredible comfort during Minnesota winters. We don't just "lay tile"—we engineer waterproof environments that increase your home's equity.

Precision Flooring

Tile flooring requires more than just a level surface; it requires a deep understanding of subfloor preparation. Whether you are choosing wood-look porcelain planks or large stone tiles, we evaluate your joist spacing and subfloor thickness to prevent future cracking. We utilize the latest leveling systems to ensure a "lippage-free" finish, meaning your floors will be perfectly flat from wall to wall. Serving the entire Twin Cities metro, we handle high-traffic entries, mudrooms, and grand foyers. Our focus is on long-term structural integrity, ensuring that the stone or porcelain you invest in today remains beautiful and crack-free for decades.
