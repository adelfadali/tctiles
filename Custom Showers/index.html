Custom Showers

100% waterproof Schluter systems for a mold-free, spa-like experience in your home.

The Gold Standard in Shower Waterproofing

A beautiful shower is useless if it leaks behind the walls. At True Craft Tile & Stone, we don't just "tile" showers; we engineer them. We exclusively use the **Schluter-Kerdi System**, an integrated family of products that creates a completely sealed, watertight envelope. Unlike traditional "hot mop" or cement board methods, our system ensures that moisture never reaches your home's framing, preventing mold and structural rot forever.

Why We Use Schluter Systems

Lifetime Reliability

The Kerdi membrane is vapor-tight, meaning even steam won't penetrate. This is essential for modern walk-in showers and steam rooms.

Precision Drainage

We install integrated Schluter-Kerdi drains that bond directly to the waterproofing, eliminating the #1 failure point in custom showers.

Investment & Planning

A custom shower is a major investment in your home's value. In the Shakopee and Minneapolis area, a professional custom shower typically ranges from **$6,000 to $15,000**, depending on the size, niche configurations, and the type of stone or porcelain used. We provide detailed CAD-style planning for every project to ensure that grout lines are centered and benches are sloped perfectly for drainage.

Zero-Entry & Curbless

We specialize in modern "curbless" designs for a seamless transition from bathroom floor to shower.

Built-in Niches & Benches

No more plastic hanging racks. We build permanent, tiled storage niches into your wall for a clean look.
