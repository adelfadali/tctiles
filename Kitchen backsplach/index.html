We prefer a high-end finish. Unless you specify otherwise, we typically use **Schluter-Systems aluminum or stainless steel profiles**. These provide a clean, modern edge that protects the tile from chipping and looks significantly better than plastic bullnose or unfinished edges.
Countertops are rarely 100% level. With 35 years of experience, we know how to "hide" these imperfections. We use **laser leveling** to establish a true horizontal line and subtly scribe the bottom row of tiles so the pattern remains visually straight across the entire kitchen.
While we are master installers, we are happy to share what we've seen work best. We can help you decide between **Herringbone, Offset, or Stacked patterns** and suggest grout colors that will either blend in for a seamless look or pop for high contrast.
