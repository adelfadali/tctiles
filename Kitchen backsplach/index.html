Elevating Your Kitchen’s Aesthetic

A kitchen backsplash is more than a functional moisture barrier; it is the visual centerpiece of your home. At True Craft Tile & Stone, we understand that the kitchen is the heart of the household, and our backsplash installations are designed to reflect that importance. Whether you prefer the timeless appeal of white subway tile or the intricate complexity of hand-laid glass mosaics, our 35+ years of experience ensures a flawless finish.

Precision is our baseline. We utilize advanced laser-leveling technology to ensure that every horizontal line is perfectly straight, even if your countertops have minor imperfections. Our master installers specialize in symmetrical layouts, ensuring that "cuts" at the corners or under cabinets are balanced and visually pleasing.

The Technical Advantage

Proper backsplash installation requires a deep understanding of substrate preparation. Many contractors skip the critical step of ensuring the drywall is flat and free of oils or grease before setting tile. We utilize premium modified thinsets that offer superior bond strength, ensuring your tiles will never shift or crack over time. For outlets and switches, we provide precision wet-saw cuts for a tight fit that is perfectly covered by your faceplates.

Waterproof Integrity

We treat the area behind the sink with extra care, ensuring silicone-based caulking at the countertop junction to prevent water from reaching the cabinets.

Zero-Mess Guarantee

We protect your countertops, flooring, and appliances with heavy-duty masking and drop cloths. Your kitchen stays clean throughout the process.
